HOW TO PLAY

Controls

  • WASD: Move
  • Mouse: Look around
  • Left Click: Shoot
  • R: Reload
  • E: Interact (plant/defuse bomb)
  • 1-5: Switch weapons
  • ESC: Pause

Objectives

  • Terrorists: Plant bomb at bombsites
  • Counter-Terrorists: Prevent bomb planting or defuse planted bombs
  • Both: Eliminate all enemy players

Weapons

  • AK-47: High damage, high recoil
  • M4A4: Balanced weapon
  • AWP: Sniper rifle, one-shot kill
  • Desert Eagle: Powerful pistol
  • Knife: Melee weapon
